Get Started with RIA

Learn how to use your intelligent voice assistant in minutes.
Step 1: Wake RIA with the Wake Word
Say “Hey RIA” to activate your assistant at any time.
Tip: Wait for the listening sound or waveform before speaking.
Step 2: Choose Your Language
Select from supported languages: English, हिंदी, தமிழ், తెలుగు, and more.
You can change your language anytime from the settings menu.
Step 3: Ask Your First Question
Try saying something like “What’s the weather today?”
RIA will respond instantly in your chosen language.
What’s the weather today?

Step 4: Use Commands
Try saying commands like:
Set a reminder
Play music
Translate this
Send a message
Step 5: Personalization Tips
RIA learns your preferences over time to serve you better.
Example: “Remind me to drink water every 2 hours.”

Frequently Asked Questions
How do I change the assistant's language?
Go to the settings menu and select your preferred language from the dropdown list. RIA will respond in the language you choose.
Can I use RIA offline?
RIA requires an internet connection for most features, such as voice recognition and real-time information.
What devices support RIA?
RIA is available on web, Android, and iOS. Check our website for the latest supported platforms.
How do I get help or support?
Use the Contact link in the top navigation or visit the Help section for support resources and FAQs.
